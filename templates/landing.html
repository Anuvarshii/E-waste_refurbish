How It Works
Step 01

Upload Device Details

Provide basic information about the device such as brand, model, battery performance, and component condition. Upload an image of the device for visual inspection.

Step 02

AI Condition Analysis

The system evaluates device condition using a combination of image-based damage detection and machine learning models to estimate refurbishment potential.

Step 03

Smart Decision Output

Based on the device condition, the system automatically recommends whether the device should be refurbished or recycled, helping reduce unnecessary electronic waste.

Advanced Features

AI Damage Detection

A convolutional neural network analyzes device images to detect visible damage and estimate physical condition.

Machine Learning Decision Engine

A trained Random Forest model evaluates refurbishment potential using physical score and damage assessment.

Sustainable E-Waste Management

Helps reduce electronic waste by identifying devices that can still be refurbished and reused.

Traceable Device Records

Each evaluated device can be recorded with its condition and decision, enabling transparent lifecycle tracking.
